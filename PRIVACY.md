# CueLine Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 6, 2026_

CueLine ("the app") is a teleprompter that displays plain text from publicly-shared Google Docs. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app does — and does not — handle.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information.**

CueLine has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. The app does not require you to sign in or provide any personal information to use it.

## How the App Works

When you paste a Google Docs URL into CueLine, the app makes a direct network request to Google's servers (`docs.google.com`) to download the plain-text contents of that document. This request happens only when you tap "Load & Start" or when Live mode refreshes.

- The document text is held only in the device's memory while you are using the app.
- The text is not transmitted to any server operated by us.
- We do not log, retain, or otherwise process the contents of your documents.

## Third-Party Services

The app communicates only with Google Docs (`docs.google.com`) at your direction, in order to fetch the plain-text version of a document you have chosen to share publicly. Your interaction with Google's services is governed by Google's own privacy policy: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>.

## Children's Privacy

CueLine is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.
